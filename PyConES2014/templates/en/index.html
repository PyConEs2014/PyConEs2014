What is PyConEs2014?

PyConEs is the national python conference
with more relevance in Spain.

PyConES second edition will be held, after the first edition's success last year in Madrid, under the mundial event PyCon's format. In this edition, the organization is by the hand of the Zaragoza Hackerspace, Dlabs, with the colaboration of the Python Spain association and the Python Zaragoza user group.

A hackerspace is a fisical place with people who has diverse interests can get to know each other, socialize and collaborate. Dlabs gives us a space where we we can meet, sharing knowledge and materials, creating some kind of open development laboratory. Right now, zaragoza's hackerspace has about twenty members who will coordinate and organise this year's event, along with many other volunteers

Zaragoza is the fifth city in spain by its size. It's enclaved halfway between madrid and barcelona, with good communications by road and high speed train (ave). It counts with a good gastronomic, nightlife and hotel offer, wich makes it the ideal city for helding big congresses.

CALL FOR PAPERS

This is for you, speackers.

Call4Papers is open for PyConES2014

Sponsorship is not yet closed, but we'll do everything that is in our hands to help with the travel and accommodation of the speackers. They'll also get a free ticket and a special gift that we'll announce at the farewell ceremony.

Types of papers

  • Talks - 50m of technical talk
  • Lighning talks - 10m of product presentation or technical talk
  • Workshops - between 50 minutes and 2h

Some important dates

  • 10 september - limit date for presenting a talk
  • 3-6 noviembre - Python course at dlabs hackerspace
  • 7 de noviembre - Special BetaBeers PyConES

If you've got a talk proposal, send us a mail to pycon2014@dlabs.co with the following:

  • Subject:   PyConEs2014 - Call4Papers Response
  • Body:   Small extract of your talk
  • Add your talk's duration
  • Tell us something about yourself

Code of conduct

All assistants are required to show respect and courtesy to the rest during the conference.
More precisely, all delegates, speakers and volunteers taking part in PyConES-related activities should adhere to the present code of conduct. Organization will enforce the fullfilment of this code of conduct.
PyConEs aims to create a discrimination free environment. Gender, sexual orientation, discapacity, fisical aspect, ethnics or religion-based discriminations will not be tolerated under any circumstance.
If any participant breaks this code of conduct, organization reserves the right to take the adecuate measures, like amonestation or expulsion without right to be reimbursed.
